My name is Ahmad Thakur. I grew up in Lahore, where I have endured the last 25 years of my existence. I am a recent Software Engineering graduate with no passion for programming anymore.

I have no idea what to do now so I have started studying philosophy and psychology. It's definitely not because of the various leftist trans and bisexual video essayists I have come across on YouTube. I want to know why people are the way they are, what makes them tick and what makes them happy. And especially, what would make me happy.

I've started writing about things I learn and my thoughts on them. I have put some of my thoughts here for the eyes of any random traveller that happens to come across this website.

I also make music when I fee like it. I get random bursts of creative spark, and when I do, I usually avoid every other adult responsibilty to just create stuff. I would like to remain in this state of mind 24/7, 365 days of the year, but alas, life is not that simple. Which is why I've started studying philosophy, to make sense of this complicated life. You can visit my YouTube to hear me talk about random stuff.
